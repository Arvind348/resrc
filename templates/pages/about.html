    what is reSRC

    reSRC stands for resource. It came to birth because I could not find a website displaying both quality curated lists and tag-sorted links.

    Whenever I found an interesting article or a tutorial on a subject I wanted to investigate, I ended up bookmarking it, together with curated lists laying here and there.

    Programmers sometimes need lists of links on a specific programming topic, be it a technology, a framework, a language, ...

    On reSRC, you are free to create your own lists or to pull the list you already have on GitHub or anywhere else. But most of you will probably be more interested by discovering great resources from great lists. Or by reSRC's tagged links database. Try Python+TDD or Ruby+Book. And of course, the meta-tag : Haskell+resource
